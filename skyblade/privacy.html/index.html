SkyBlade Privacy Policy

This policy explains how BlueSkyStudio handles data for SkyBlade, including app analytics, optional Google Play Games features, optional Google Play Billing purchases, support requests, security, retention, and deletion.

1. Scope and Developer Information

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game SkyBlade, package name com.blueskystudio.skyblade, published by BlueSkyStudio.

SkyBlade is an arcade shooter. The game does not require a BlueSkyStudio account to play. Some platform features may be available through Google services, such as Google Play Games Services for achievements or leaderboards and Google Play Billing for purchases.

Developer: BlueSkyStudio

Privacy contact: lengyy6@gmail.com

Website: This GitHub Pages site is the official website and privacy policy host for SkyBlade.

2. Data Safety Summary

SkyBlade is designed to collect only the data needed to operate, maintain, secure, and improve the game and optional Google Play features.

  • No sale of personal information: BlueSkyStudio does not sell personal or sensitive user data.
  • No sensitive device permissions: SkyBlade does not request access to precise location, camera, microphone, contacts, SMS, call logs, calendar, or files outside normal app storage.
  • No BlueSkyStudio account: The game does not create a separate developer account. If Google Play Games is enabled and you sign in, that sign-in is handled by Google.
  • Privacy policy availability: This page is publicly accessible as HTML and is intended for the Google Play privacy policy URL.

The exact data processed depends on the version of SkyBlade you use and which optional platform features are enabled.

4. How We Use Data

BlueSkyStudio uses data for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain SkyBlade's core gameplay and settings.
  • Save progress locally and restore optional purchase entitlements.
  • Operate optional Google Play Games achievements and leaderboards.
  • Analyze game performance, stability, and gameplay usage when analytics is enabled.
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, technical errors, and security issues.
  • Respond to support, privacy, and data deletion requests.
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, tax, accounting, and Google Play policy obligations.

5. Sharing and Third-Party Service Providers

BlueSkyStudio does not sell personal or sensitive user data. We may share or make data available only as described in this policy:

  • Google Firebase / Google Analytics for Firebase: analytics and diagnostics data used to understand app performance and improve the game.
  • Google Play Games Services: optional sign-in, achievements, leaderboard, and profile-related features.
  • Google Play Billing: optional purchase processing, entitlement management, refunds, and fraud prevention.
  • Legal and safety reasons: when required by law, valid legal process, security investigation, or to protect rights, users, and the public.
  • Business transfer: if SkyBlade or BlueSkyStudio assets are transferred, data may be transferred with appropriate notice where required.

Third-party providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies. You can review Google's privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy and Firebase privacy information at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards appropriate for a mobile game, including access controls, platform security features, and encrypted transport where data is transmitted by Google or service-provider SDKs.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. If we become aware of a security issue that affects your personal information, we will take appropriate steps consistent with applicable law and platform requirements.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer period is required for legal, security, tax, accounting, or dispute-resolution reasons.

  • Local gameplay data: stored on your device until you delete it, clear app data, or uninstall SkyBlade.
  • Analytics data: retained according to the Firebase / Google Analytics retention settings used for the project.
  • Purchase records: retained as needed to verify entitlements, process refunds, prevent fraud, and meet legal or accounting obligations.
  • Support messages: retained for the time needed to respond and maintain a record of the request.

SkyBlade does not currently create a separate BlueSkyStudio user account. To request deletion of data that BlueSkyStudio can reasonably access, contact lengyy6@gmail.com with the subject line "SkyBlade Data Deletion Request". We may need information from you to verify and process the request.

For Google Play Games profile data, Google account data, Google Play purchase history, or data controlled directly by Google, use the privacy controls provided by Google for your account and Google Play services.

8. Your Choices and Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to, or receive a copy of personal information. You may also have the right to withdraw consent where processing depends on consent.

You can exercise available rights by contacting lengyy6@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable period required by applicable law and may ask for verification information before fulfilling a request.

  • You can clear local game data through your device's app settings.
  • You can uninstall SkyBlade to remove the game and local data from your device.
  • You can manage Google Play Games and Google account privacy settings through Google's account tools.
  • You can manage Google Play purchases and refunds through Google Play support channels.

9. Children's Privacy

SkyBlade is not directed to children under 13 or to children under the minimum age required by local law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to BlueSkyStudio through SkyBlade or support communications, contact us at lengyy6@gmail.com. We will take reasonable steps to review and delete the information where required.

10. International Transfers

SkyBlade may be offered globally. Data processed by Google Firebase, Google Play Games, Google Play Billing, email, or support providers may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We rely on service-provider safeguards and applicable legal mechanisms where required.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when SkyBlade, service providers, legal requirements, or Google Play policy requirements change. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page shows when the policy was most recently revised.

If a change is material, we will provide notice through the app, this website, the Google Play listing, or another appropriate method where required.
